Nicéphore Niépce

The Father of Photography

Here is a time line of Nicéphore's life:

  • 1765 - Born in Chalon-sur-Saône, France
  • 1793 - Invents the Pyréolophore, the world's first internal combustion engine
  • 1826 - Produces the world's first photograph
  • 1833 - Dies in Saint-Loup-de-Varennes, France

If you have time, you should read more about this incredible human being on his Wikipedia entry.
